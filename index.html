Coffee

Expresso


Flavor-Profile

Bold, rich, and concentrated with a slightly bitter taste.


Characteristics

Served in small shots, forms the base for many coffee drinks, has a thick crema on top.

Cappuccino


Flavor-Profile

Rich, creamy, and slightly sweet with a balanced espresso flavor.


Characteristics

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam; often topped with cocoa or cinnamon.

Americano


Flavor-Profile

Smooth, rich coffee flavor with a diluted intensity.


Characteristics

Made by adding hot water to espresso, producing a coffee similar to brewed coffee.

Latte


Flavor-Profile

Smooth and creamy with a mild coffee flavor, often sweetened.


Characteristics

Made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Macchiato


Flavor-Profile

Strong coffee flavor with a hint of sweetness from the milk.


Characteristics

Espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or foam, can be served iced or hot.

Mocha


Flavor-Profile

Rich and chocolatey with a sweet coffee flavor.


Characteristics

Made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, often topped with whipped cream.

Cold Coffee

Iced Coffee


Flavor-Profile

Refreshing, slightly sweet, and smooth coffee flavor.


Characteristics

Brewed and chilled coffee, often served over ice, can be sweetened or flavored.

Cold Brew Coffee


Flavor-Profile

Smooth, mellow, and less acidic with chocolate and nutty notes.


Characteristics

Made by steeping coarsely ground coffee in cold water for an extended period i.e; served chilled.

Frappuccino


Flavor-Profile

Creamy and sweet with a blend of coffee and flavors like chocolate or caramel.


Characteristics

Blended iced coffee drink, often topped with whipped cream, available in various flavors.

Iced Latte


Flavor-Profile

Creamy and smooth with a balanced coffee flavor, slightly sweet.


Characteristics

Made with espresso, cold milk, and ice; can be sweetened or flavored.

Iced Mocha


Flavor-Profile

Rich, chocolatey, and coffee-flavored with a sweet finish.


Characteristics

Iced latte with chocolate syrup, often topped with whipped cream and chocolate drizzle.

Affogato


Flavor-Profile

Deliciously sweet and creamy with a strong coffee flavor.


Characteristics

A scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato "drowned" in a shot of hot espresso; a dessert-style drink.
